Yahan tumhein theek hone ki zaroorat nahi.
Aur strong dikhne ki bhi nahi.
Bas khud ko kuch der ke liye chhodo, gehri saansen lo, aur bas theher jao.
Kabhi kabhi insaan itna kuch seh leta hai, ke sehna hi aadat ban jata hai.
Phir ek din dil bolna chhod deta hai.
Iska matlab ye nahi ke dil gaya… bas wo thak sa gaya hota hai, ruk sa gaya hota hai.
Agar zehen shor karta hai, to usey chup karwana zaroori nahi.
Shor aksar thakawat hoti hai, khatra nahi.
Aur thakawat ko solve nahi kiya jata… bas araam diya jata hai.
Kuch pal ke liye waqt ko dheema karte hain.
Kisi cheez ka peecha nahi.
Aur kisi cheez se bhaagna bhi nahi.
Tum se koi sawal nahi poocha ja raha.
Na koi jawab chahiye.
Bas itni si request hai… MUSKURAO.
Muskurao… kyunki tumne ye sab sah liya.
Muskurao… kyunki tumne har challenge ko face kiya.
Muskurao… kyunki tum iske layak ho.
In saloon mein tumne apni muskaan kayi baar chhupayi hogi.
Aur kayi cheezain tum par haavi hoti hongi.
Phir bhi… tumne haar nahi mani, akele giri, akele sambhali, akele ladi.
Aaj khud ko shabashi do.
Dard ko mehsoos karna bhi zaroori hai, lekin aaj kuch extra pyaar khud ko do.
Ab shayad tumhara dil thoda saah liya ho.
Tum aaj use chhuno, thoda pyaar aur maafi do.
Chalo… khud ko ek chhoti party do.
Khud ko apni yaadon ka ek doura do.
Aankhen band karo aur un moments ko recall karo:
Jinmein tumne zindagi khul ke jii, jinmein tumhara dil khushi se hasa, jin moments mein tum sirf wahi thi.
Aaj unko dubara jiyo… har ehsaas ke saath.
Jab aankhen khol lo, bas ek kaam aur karo.
Khud ko ek baar tasalli se dekho sheeshay mein.
Jo shakhs samne hai, use honestly jawab do… bina dare, bina shor ke.
Agar tum yahan tak aa gaye ho…
To bas ye yaad rakho:
Jo baadal tumhare raaste mein aaye… unka andhera sirf ek pal ke liye hai, Tum sooraj hou… tumhari roshni hamesha apni jagah hai.
Jate jate bas ye yaad rakhna…
Kuch mojoodgian shor nahi karti.
Wo sirf sath hoti hain… har pal, bina shor ke, bas sath.
